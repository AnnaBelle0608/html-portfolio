Ashleigh's Favorite Movies

My favorite go-to movies

Boondock Saints

A great movie about two irish brothers who accidentally fall into a new line of work with some great banter and one liners.

Under The Tuscan Sun

A great love story about an author who spontaneously moves from the US to Italy after a divorce and finds herself and wonderful new friends.

McLintock

An old American Western featuring John Wayne and Maureen O'Hara

The Proposal

Cute RomCom with Sandra Bullock, Ryan Reynolds and Betty White
